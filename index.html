Goku

About Goku

Goku is a Saiyan who was sent to Earth as a baby with the mission to destroy it. However, after suffering a head injury, he lost his aggressive nature and became one of Earth's greatest defenders. Goku is known for his cheerful and energetic personality, as well as his insatiable appetite and love for fighting strong opponents. He is also fiercely loyal to his friends and family, often putting their safety above his own. Throughout the Dragon Ball series, Goku undergoes numerous transformations, each more powerful than the last. His most iconic transformation is the Super Saiyan form, which he first achieves during his battle with Frieza on Planet Namek. Goku's character has become a cultural icon, inspiring countless fans around the world with his determination, courage, and unwavering spirit. He continues to be a beloved figure in the world of anime and manga.
